Global Somali Bantu Council

A united Somali Bantu voice across borders.

GSBC serves as the official umbrella body for Somali Bantu communities in Somalia, refugee camps, and the diaspora.

From riverine villages to resettlement cities, we work to defend rights, protect identity, and build opportunity for the next generation.
